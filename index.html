about SOS

The organization SOS was established in 2007 for the common good of the less priviledge and the poor chidren, It is hard at work in communities across USA and other part of the world. Save our souls foundation(SOS) was founded to nurture and improve the quality of life of needy poor children.The foundation was named in honor of children who are dying from preventable diseases.

Mission causes

The mission of Save our Soul foundation is to foster a positive diffference in the lives of needy children, primarily through activities and services to help them achieve their highest potentials and become caring individuals.

what we stand for

We value children's life and embrace the belief that no child should die from preventable diseases like malaria,waterborne ,illnesses,measles ,polio ,tuberculosis and HIV/AIDS

where we are going

Our vision is to end the cradle to death of children in inner city America and afica from preventable diseases through the provison of good health , water , sanitation , education and sustainable livelihoods
